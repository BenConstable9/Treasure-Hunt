Ram Bar

The Ram Bar provides you with the ultimate stylish and comfortable surroundings to meet up, unwind, or get ready to go out. Bringing you freshly prepared pub grub and friendly service, the Ram Bar is your social hub on campus. From regular quiz nights and Rameoke, to pre-Lemmy bar on Saturdays we have something for everyone. The only place on campus showing live sport with 6 screens and a large projector, the Ram is the place to catch all the live action each weekend. So come in, choose your table – inside or in our lively beer garden – and join us.

Events:

  • Monday - Quiz Night: Join us for quiz night every Monday, organised by RAG. Bring your mates, grab a table and put your skills on show.
  • Thursday - Ram Jam: Our weekly acoustic night showcasing the best student talent on campus.
  • Friday - Rameoke: Our infamous weekly karaoke night. Whether solo, duet, or with everyone you know, there's a tune for you.

Opening and closing time: 10:00 - 23:00
